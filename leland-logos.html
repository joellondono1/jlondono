Leland Logo (demos)

Leland Management decided to redesign their logo in 2019 and asked that I come up with a few designs. I decided to go with a modern, abstract, and bright design, while incorporating the main aspects and colors of their old logo. In addition to my designs, they asked a design agency to work on some as well. Unfortunately, they did not go with my designs; however, I enjoyed working on them and continue doing designs for the company.

I specialize in pictoral and abstract logos. Check out other designs I have created for other clients; including a patent logo and web show logo!
