Double Vision Show

Double Vision is a web talk show/podcast that talks about all the aspects of life. The hosts of the show are from two different backgrounds, yet they agree on a lot things. In this logo, the subtle details express the idea behind the show. The color swap on each of the lenses represents the different culture and the common ground they often find. In addition to the color swap, the words are spelled out on the opposite sides to express their opposing views. The mustaches is a symbol of chivalry and respect, symbolizing the ability to agree to disagree. And lastly, they both wear gleasses, so there is that.

Double Vision is fairly new so they don't have a website yet. Plans are in the works to make it happen soon. For now, I invite you to take a look my other projects, and see what I can do for you and your business.
