The Piggyback

The Piggyback is a laser angle square adapter invented by Wayne Graddy. The logo was designed and drawn by his son Dylan. Wayne entrusted me to create a digital version of the sketch. Wayne instructed me that he wanted to stick to the original drawing as much as possible. So I made sure to get every notch on the ruler and every detail on the pigs. After I had completed the sketch, I added in the colors that Wayne chose to help it "pop." I really enjoyed being able to bring Dylan's work to life.
