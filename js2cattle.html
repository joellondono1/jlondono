JS2 Cattle Co.

This website was designed to share the story of a humble family owned farm. It showcases what makes them special and their products. Since it's a website they plan on updating regularly, they decided to use squarespace. With an intuitive builder and some custom code, js2cattle.com was born. The website features a simple color scheme and let's the images speak for itself. The site is also fully responsive across all devices.
