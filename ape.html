APE Pest Control

APE Pest control is a small business from Fort Myers, FL. This project taught me a lot about listening to the client and building off of ideas. To start, I had a rough sketch and some other ideas to go off of. After the first draft we sat down and picked apart what worked and what didn't. The main idea was to make a logo that was strong, supportive, and friendly. In this project, it was all in the subtle details. The muscles represent the dependability, while the smile represents the friendly service that makes APE!
